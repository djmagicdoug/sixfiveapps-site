How it works

From conversation to live in days.

No lengthy onboarding. No back-and-forth for weeks. You give us the information, we do the rest.

We have a quick conversation

Tell us about your business — what you do, who you serve, what you want your customers to be able to do. 20 minutes is usually enough.

You fill out the intake form

Hours, contact info, menu or services, photos, links — we send you a form built around your hub. You fill it in at your pace.

We build and deliver

Your hub is built, designed, and tested. We send you a link and a QR code. Most builds are live within a few business days.

You share it everywhere

Business cards, receipts, email signatures, table tents, social bios — anywhere your customers might look, your hub is one tap away.
